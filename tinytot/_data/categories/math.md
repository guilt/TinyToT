## Chain: Half and Fraction Arithmetic
<!-- Handles: half of, what is half, half of a number, what number, halve, divide by two, divide by 2, fraction of, one half, one quarter, one third -->
Thought 1: "Half of X" means divide X by 2. "Half of 250" = 250 ÷ 2 = 125.
Thought 2: "Half of what number is Y?" means Y × 2 = answer. Example: half of 250 is 125.
Thought 3: For fractions: 1/4 of X = X ÷ 4; 1/3 of X = X ÷ 3; half = divide by 2.
Conclusion: Half of a number means dividing it by 2. Half of 250 = 125. Half of 100 = 50. Half = divide by 2. A third = divide by 3. A quarter = divide by 4. Two-thirds: multiply by 2 then divide by 3.

## Chain 5: Circle Area Calculation
<!-- Handles: circle, area, radius, geometry, find the area, find area of circle, area of a circle, calculate area circle, circumference circle -->
Thought 1: Find the area of a circle: Area = π × r² where r is the radius. Find the area of a circle with radius 5: Area = π × 5².
Thought 2: π ≈ 3.14159. Find the area: 5² = 25. Area = 3.14159 × 25 ≈ 78.54 square units.
Thought 3: To find the area of a circle with any radius r: multiply π by r squared. The circumference of a circle = 2πr.
Conclusion: Find the area of a circle: A = πr². For radius 5: A = π × 25 ≈ 78.54. For radius 3: A = π × 9 ≈ 28.27. Circumference = 2πr.
